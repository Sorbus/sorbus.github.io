Aucuparia

Aucuparia - n. A deciduous tree in the Rosaceae family, often called the rowan or mountain-ash, more properly referred to by its full binomial name, Sorbus aucuparia. Lit. "birdcatcher", from avis (latin, "bird") and capere (latin, present infinitive form of capio, "to capture"), named as such because of the use of its fruit as bait for fowling.
